Additional Information:

  • Commiters Limit Explained

    Registration key you've obtained may set limit on a number of committers. SubGit takes into account number of distinct committers in the Git history, that, upon initial Subversion to Git translation is completed, will also include all committers from the Subversion project history. To make sure there are no semantic duplicates, you need to keep your authors mapping up-to-date. In case number of distinct committers exceeds the limit, SubGit will start to issue friendly reminders about the necessity to upgrade your registration key; otherwise SubGit mirror will remain fully functional.

  • Awesome Professional Support

    Our awesome professional support guarantees you one business day reply to your questions on SubGit configuration and installations as well as highest priority with fixing issues you report. Awesomeness doesn't stop on that - upon your request support could be upgraded to 12-hours guaranteed reply or even to the instant online assistance to make sure your migration with SubGit is as smooth as possible.
